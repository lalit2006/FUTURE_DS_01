**📊 E-commerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)**

An interactive dashboard designed to analyze e-commerce performance, identifying key revenue drivers and sales trends to support data-driven decision-making.

---

**🎯 Project Objectives**

Identify top-performing products and categories.

Analyze sales and profit trends over time.

Visualize regional performance and volume metrics.

---

**🛠 Tech Stack**

Tool: Power BI

Language: DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

Data Source: Microsoft Excel (SuperStore Dataset)

---

**📈 Key Features**

KPI Overview: High-level metrics for Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Orders.

Trend Analysis: Line charts showing growth over time.

Product Performance: Bar charts of Top 10 products and Pie charts for category distribution.

Dynamic Filters: Interactive slicers for Date, Category, and Region.

---

**💡 Business Insights**

Top Category: Technology generates the highest overall sales.

Regional Lead: The West region is the primary revenue contributor.

Growth: Sales show a consistent upward trajectory.


Concentration: A small group of "Top 10" products drives a significant portion of total revenue.

---

**🚀 Skills Demonstrated**

Data Modeling: Connecting and cleaning complex datasets.

DAX measures: Creating custom calculations for business metrics.

Visualization: Designing intuitive, user-friendly reports.
